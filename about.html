About Solvex

A legacy of excellence in paint manufacturing by Enova Industries

Our Story

Founded over 7 years ago, Solvex by Enova Industries has been at the forefront of paint innovation in Pakistan. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in the paint industry.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for homeowners, architects, and contractors across the country.

Today, we continue to set new standards in the industry with our eco-friendly formulations and cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality paints and coatings that protect, beautify, and enhance every surface while maintaining our commitment to environmental sustainability and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the leading paint manufacturer in India, known for innovation, quality, and environmental responsibility. We envision a world where every surface tells a story of beauty and protection.

Our Values

Quality Excellence

Sustainability
